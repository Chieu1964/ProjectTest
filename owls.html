Making a Sandwhich

  1. Choose bread for sandwhich
  2. Choose meats for sandwhich
  3. Choose condiments for sandwhich
  4. Take the condiment and open the lid
  5. Squeeze the bottle on one side of the bread
  6. Spread the condiment with a knife all over the bread
  7. Place the chosen meat on the bread until it equals 0.25 cm thick
  8. Place the other slice of bread on top of the meat
  9. Your sandwhich is done
